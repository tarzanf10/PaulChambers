 Freeview Switchover

In the UK, the switchover from analogue to digital TV started on 17 October 2007 and was completed on 24 October 2012. Each group of regional transmitters had its analogue broadcasts switched off at a certain point between those dates. Although this was some time ago the companies keep adding & changing the channel Numbers that thay use. See links below to help you keep your equipment up-to-date with channels
